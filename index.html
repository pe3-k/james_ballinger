Something about me

James Ballinger Limited is a Midland-based carpentry & building company with xx years of experience. We provide all aspects of carpentry and building for both domestic and commercial sectors within the Midlands. We are Vetted, Reveiwed & Approved by the Federation of Master Builders, Trustmark, Checkatrade & Trustatrader. We take pride in everything we do and aim for nothing more than outstanding results on time and on budget that will make James Ballinger Ltd. stand out from the rest. Customer satisfaction is always of the utmost importance. We will help design, build & produce anything in the form of traditional or contemporary style carpentry and joinery, using our own CNC technology we are able to create all aspects of bespoke joinery. Also kitchens, House renovations, and much more. We work to build your desired vision, using what inspires you, and what styles that best join together with the rest of your project. With our bespoke approach, everything is possible. Whether you’re looking for a classic design that fits seamlessly into your period home, or a contemporary style for a more modern approach, we can design, build and transform your space into the vision you’ve always wanted.
